About the Union

What if the Soviet Union never collapsed?

In our alternative timeline, the reforms of the late 1980s took a different path. The Union adapted, evolved, and survived into the modern era. Our Discord server simulates this living, breathing government — complete with elected officials, legislative debates, and geopolitical roleplay.

This is not just a chat server. It's a community-driven political simulation where every citizen can rise through the ranks, propose legislation, serve in the Politburo, or shape foreign policy. We blend historical accuracy with creative worldbuilding.

Government Structure

Organs of State Power

The government operates through a structured hierarchy of councils and committees, each with defined responsibilities and elected or appointed officials.

⭐
Supreme Leadership

The Politburo

The highest decision-making body of the Communist Party. Members shape policy, approve decrees, and guide the ideological direction of the Union. Led by the General Secretary.

🏛️
Legislative Body

Supreme Soviet

The bicameral legislature comprising the Soviet of the Union and the Soviet of Nationalities. Debates and ratifies all federal legislation and constitutional amendments.

⚖️
Executive Power

Council of Ministers

The executive branch responsible for implementing legislation and managing state affairs. Each minister oversees a specific portfolio — from defense to agriculture.

🛡️
State Security

Committee for State Security

Responsible for intelligence, counterintelligence, and ensuring the security of the state. Operates both domestically and in the international arena through dedicated directorates.

🌍
International Relations

Ministry of Foreign Affairs

Manages diplomatic relations, treaties, and international cooperation. Coordinates with other mock governments and maintains the Union's position on the world stage.

📜
Constitutional Oversight

Presidium

The permanent body of the Supreme Soviet that acts on its behalf between sessions. Issues decrees, interprets laws, and represents the Union in ceremonial matters.

Alternative Timeline

History of the Enduring Union

Key moments in our alternative history where the USSR charted a different course.

1991 — The Turning Point

Reform Prevails Over Collapse

Rather than dissolving, the Union enacts sweeping but controlled economic reforms. The "August Compromise" preserves the federal structure while granting greater autonomy to individual republics. A new Union Treaty is signed by all 15 republics.

1997 — Economic Renaissance

The Soviet Market Reforms

A hybrid socialist-market economy takes shape. State enterprises are modernized, cooperative businesses flourish, and the ruble stabilizes. The Union becomes a major player in the emerging global tech economy.

2004 — Digital Revolution

SOVNET Goes Online

The Soviet internet — SOVNET — launches as a state-backed but increasingly open network. Soviet engineers and programmers become globally competitive, and Minsk emerges as the "Silicon Valley of the East."

2014 — New World Order

The Eurasian Accords

The USSR brokers a historic agreement with the European Union and China, establishing new frameworks for trade and arms reduction. The Cold War officially transitions into an era of "Competitive Cooperation."

2024 — Present Day

A Union Reborn

The modern USSR stands as a global superpower alongside the United States and China. With a population of 310 million, a space program on Mars, and a thriving cultural renaissance, the Union looks forward to its next century.
